Mirror check

Physical meaning

Camber creates lateral tire force. Toe is the small slip-angle correction that can counter a left-right camber-thrust imbalance. Caster enters as a steering-axis and straight-ahead reference bias, so the same dC term can move both axle split targets.

Camber-caster cancellation appears when dm and dC point in opposite signs: the tire-force imbalance and steering-reference imbalance partially subtract before the split-toe correction is calculated.
